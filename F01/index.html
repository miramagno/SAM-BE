Philippine Medalists

Celebrate the remarkable achievements of Filipino athletes in the Olympic Games.

Hidilyn Diaz

Sport: Weightlifting

Achievement: Gold Medal in Women's 55kg Weightlifting (Tokyo 2020)

Hidilyn Diaz made history becoming the first Filipino athlete to win an Olympic gold medal, bringing pride to the nation in the 2020 Tokyo Olympics.

Nesthy Petecio

Sport: Boxing

Achievement: Silver Medal in Women's Featherweight Boxing (Tokyo 2020)

Nesthy Petecio showcased her talent and resilience, winning a silver medal in women's boxing at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics.

Carlo Paalam

Sport: Boxing

Achievement: Silver Medal in Men's Flyweight Boxing (Tokyo 2020)

Carlo Paalam earned a silver medal in boxing at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics, further cementing the Philippines' strong presence in combat sports.

Eumir Marcial

Sport: Boxing

Achievement: Bronze Medal in Men's Middleweight Boxing (Tokyo 2020)

Eumir Marcial clinched a bronze medal in boxing at the Tokyo 2020 Olympics, adding another historic win for Filipino athletes.

Margielyn Didal

Sport: Skateboarding

Achievement: 7th Place in Women's Street Skateboarding (Tokyo 2020)

Margielyn Didal brought skateboarding to the global stage and inspired young Filipinos with her strong Olympic performance.

Alex Eala

Sport: Tennis

Achievement: 2022 US Open Girls' Singles Champion

Alex Eala is a rising star in tennis, representing the Philippines with her skill and determination on the international stage.
